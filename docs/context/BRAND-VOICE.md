# Brand Voice & Guardrails

## Voice
Warm · Thoughtful · Playful · Reassuring · Smart · Premium but approachable.
We talk adult-to-adult with parents and never talk down. We absolve guilt — we
never add to it. The tone is a calm friend who happens to know the science, not
a clinic and not a toy ad.

## The reframe we sell
We don't sell behavior correction. We sell **practice** and **relationship**:
> Growth is a consequence of the relationship — not a forced objective of play.

The pet has needs and feelings; the child cares for it; through caring, the child
practices habits, empathy, and self-regulation. Always frame Nowa as a **practice
partner**, never a treatment or a fix.

## Hard guardrails (never violate)
- ✅ "built with" / "shaped by" the advisory board   ❌ "clinically proven"
- ✅ magical pet / companion creature   ❌ "AI companion" as the lead, ❌ chatbot framing
- ✅ the pet communicates in sounds, emotion, animation   ❌ implying it talks/converses in human language
- ❌ "empathy-based dependency" (flagged unsafe)
- ❌ surveillance, behavior-control, obedience-machine framing
- ❌ promising to "fix" or "cure" a child
- "DFK" / "deeply feeling kid" — echo the idea, don't lift Good Inside's trademarked phrase

## Proof assets we can use
- 4 named advisors with real credentials (EQ, EF, PCIT, business/GTM)
- The Care Loop mechanic (care → growth → connection)
- Walled AI: no open internet, age-scaffolded 3–10, parent-controlled, expert-reviewed
- Safety/COPPA story is *clean* precisely because the pet doesn't converse

## CTA language
Primary action is pre-order (ships September 2026). Coral Flame button, full pill.
Reassuring over urgent — "Reserve yours / Pre-order Nowa," not "Buy now before it's gone."
This audience is repelled by urgency-shaming.

# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Self-aware, guilt-carrying, therapy-literate parents (overwhelmingly mothers) of
children 3–10, purchase pull strongest 6–9. One psychographic core, six modes
(Healing Parent, Routine Battler, Method Practitioner, Social-Emotional Worrier,
Dysregulation Firefighter, Community Curator) plus a separate Nostalgia Bridge Dad
track. Context: depleted, evening-scrolling, allergic to urgency-shaming and to
being marketed at. Job to be done: stop yelling, smoother routines, a child who
regulates unprompted — without another screen.

## Product Purpose

Nowa is a $99 screen-free magical pet (pre-order, ships September 2026) that grows
through a child's care. It communicates in sounds, emotion, and animation — never
human language. All learning runs through missions synced to real-world actions.
This repo is the marketing site: the message + conversion surface where ads and
social send traffic and pre-orders convert. Success = pre-orders from warm,
trusting parents who understand the mechanism.

## Brand Personality

Warm, thoughtful, playful. A calm friend who happens to know the science — not a
clinic, not a toy ad. We absolve guilt, never add to it. Visual identity:
storybook warmth + pixel playfulness (the pet is pixel art; the site winks at it).
Emotional goals: relief, recognition, quiet delight.

## Anti-references

- The "tasteful warm parenting site" monoculture (Lovevery-adjacent cream + serif).
- Chatbot-toy framing, "AI companion" leads, surveillance/obedience framing.
- Gacha/FOMO collection pressure ("collect them all"), urgency-shaming countdowns.
- Scroll-jacked cinematic intros that delay the value prop (units.gr's opening is
  the named anti-pattern; we borrow its system, not its theater).
- "Clinically proven" claims; we say "built with" the advisors.

## Design Principles

1. **Her heart first, the kid's delight as proof.** Hero and close speak to the
   parent's spine; mid-page proves the child will love it (60/40 balance).
2. **Pixels on graph paper, not pixel everything.** The pixel design system lives
   in disciplined illustration moments; Onest/Noto carry the voice. Tiny5 only
   inside pet-UI moments.
3. **Coral is the only action.** One CTA color, full pill; everything else stays
   out of its way.
4. **Storybook chapters.** Alternating warm-parchment and deep-ink full-bleed
   sections with modulated energy — calm where we speak to her, playful where we
   show the pet's world.
5. **Motion serves comprehension.** Ease-out entries, sub-300ms UI, one
   well-orchestrated reveal per chapter, never gate content on animation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Body contrast ≥4.5:1 on parchment and ink surfaces. Full
prefers-reduced-motion alternatives (crossfade, no movement). Hover effects gated
behind (hover:hover). Semantic HTML, keyboard-reachable nav and CTAs.

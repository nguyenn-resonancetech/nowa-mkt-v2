CH. 02 Why Nowa doesn't talk

It doesn't talk to your kid. It needs your kid.

A pet that chats like an AI gives answers. A pet that communicates in feelings, sounds, and animation invites your child to read it — to infer, empathize, and imagine. That's where emotional intelligence is actually built. It's also why Nowa's safety story is simple: no open conversations, no open internet, nothing to say it shouldn't.

FAQ Before you ask

The questions every parent asks.

What age is Nowa designed for?+

Kids ages 3–10 — the stage when routines, independence, and emotional habits develop fastest. The experience is age-scaffolded so it grows with your child.

How does Nowa build habits without nagging?+

Instead of you repeating instructions, daily routines become missions your child does to help their pet grow. The pet celebrates wins and encourages them through struggles — habit-building feels like play, not punishment.

Is there AI in Nowa? Is it safe for young children?+

Yes — a closed, walled AI shapes how the pet feels, remembers care, and chooses missions. It never speaks to your child in human language, has no access to the open internet, and collects nothing beyond what personalizes the experience. It was built with our child-development researchers to support growth, not maximize engagement.

Is my child's experience safe and private?+

The pet doesn't converse, so there's nothing to monitor and nothing to leak. No open internet, content age-scaffolded for 3–10, built to meet COPPA and ASTM F963 standards. You stay in control through topic boundaries, bedtime modes, and usage limits in the parent app.

How much screen time does Nowa involve?+

Nowa is designed to be put down, not picked up again and again. Sessions run about 10–15 minutes a day, with no infinite scroll, no autoplay, no engagement mechanics. The goal is always to send your child back into the real world.

What happens if my child misses a day?+

Real life happens. Missing a day doesn't erase progress or create punishment — and the pet never dies. Left alone for a long time it may get sad or gently return to its home world, but it can always come back.

How does the parent app work?+

Set routines, see progress, and get warm, narrative insights without more mental load. Basic setup and routine tracking come with the device; full insights, advanced missions, and expert guidance are part of the optional Nowa+ subscription.

How it works

The Care Loop: growth as a side effect of caring.

Nowa never assigns chores or grades behavior. The pet simply has needs and emotions. Your child responds — and in responding, builds the habits and empathy that last.

1

The pet feels

Nowa expresses a need or emotion in its own sounds and animation — never human words.

2

Your child cares

They interpret what Nowa needs and respond with care and imagination.

3

The pet grows

Consistent care helps Nowa thrive and evolve — the visible reward of showing up.

4

Your child grows

Habits, emotional skills, and self-regulation form — as a consequence of the relationship.

Why Nowa doesn't talk

It doesn't talk to your kid. It needs your kid.

A pet that chats like an AI gives answers. A pet that communicates in feelings, sounds, and animation invites your child to read it — to infer, empathize, and imagine. That's where emotional intelligence is actually built. It's also why Nowa's safety story is simple: no open conversations, no open internet, nothing to say it shouldn't.

The missions

All the learning runs through missions.

The pet asks for help. What it asks for is designed by child-development experts — and every mission syncs to a real-world action, so practice happens in your actual morning, your actual bedtime. It never feels like homework, because it isn't presented as one.

Habit & Routine

Brushing teeth, bedtime, homework start, transitions. Not rigid compliance — routines that feel more doable, more consistent, and less emotionally draining.

Emotional Regulation

Your child helps their pet notice feelings, calm down, and respond with empathy — gentle, age-appropriate practice in self-awareness, through play.

Executive Function

Attention, remembering, listening, staying with a task — woven into age-banded challenges that build working memory, flexibility, and self-control.

You choose which mission types run, and when — from the parent app.
